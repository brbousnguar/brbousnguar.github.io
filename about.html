About Me

My journey from a fresh engineering graduate to a seasoned SAP Commerce Cloud consultant and Mulesoft integrator.

I How did your career journey begin?

I started my career journey right after graduating with my State Engineer degree in Computer Science & BI from INSEA Rabat in 2015. My first step into the professional world was an internship at Brake.fr, where I was introduced to SAP Commerce Cloud (then called Hybris).

What began as learning about product import flows and CMS customizations quickly evolved into a deep fascination with e-commerce architecture. Over the years, I became curious about how complex enterprise systems integrate and communicate, leading me to master not just the technical aspects but also the business logic behind modern e-commerce platforms.

II What drives your passion for technology?

I'm passionate about building robust, scalable integration solutions that connect complex enterprise systems seamlessly. My expertise in SAP Commerce Cloud combined with my recent transition to Mulesoft allows me to bridge the gap between traditional e-commerce platforms and modern integration architectures.

I'm a lifelong learner who thrives on staying current with emerging technologies. Whether it's mastering DataWeave transformations, implementing microservices with Kubernetes, or optimizing API performance, I love diving deep into technical challenges and sharing knowledge with my team.

What drives me most is the opportunity to mentor junior developers and contribute to architectural decisions that have long-term impact on business success.

III What's next in your professional journey?

I'm currently seeking opportunities that leverage my unique combination of SAP Commerce Cloud expertise and Mulesoft integration skills. I'm particularly interested in roles that allow me to:

  • Lead complex integration projects
  • Architect scalable e-commerce solutions
  • Mentor development teams
  • Drive cloud-native transformations

With 8+ years of experience in enterprise e-commerce and a proven track record of successful migrations and integrations, I'm ready to take on new challenges that push the boundaries of what's possible in modern commerce technology.
